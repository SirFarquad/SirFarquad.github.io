Swimming

I started swimming at 7 when my dad first took me swimming at our local Marriott hotel pool. He just wanted me to do a sport with a low risk of injury, but I soon fell in love with it. I was with Scarlet Aquatics briefly for a year, but switched to the Wyckoff Family YMCA Sharks and swam with them from 8 to 18. Thank you to Coach Peta for putting up with me all those years. These were some of the best times of my life and it is through the sport that I have met some lifelong friends. I currently swim for Illini Swim Club, and it has been a blast so far!
